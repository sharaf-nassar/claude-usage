My recommendation: A (2×2 Grid)

Best cockpit metaphor. All metrics visible without scrolling. Each chart gets its own axis scale without confusion. The grid scales well in a Tauri window sidebar panel. Option B creates a false hierarchy, and C wastes vertical space on 4 charts that share the same time axis.
