Synchronized Stack

Why this works
Full width per chart — maximizes time-series resolution in narrow panel.
Synced crosshair — correlate events across metrics at the same timestamp.
Shared axis — eliminates redundant labels, saves vertical space.
Proven pattern — Grafana, Datadog, and every monitoring tool uses this.
B
Expandable Accordion
Compact rows with sparkline previews. Click to expand one chart at full height. Collapsed rows show current value + trend. Saves space when you only need one chart.

Trade-offs
Pro: Most compact — collapsed items use ~32px each. Great if you usually focus on one metric.
Con: Can't correlate across charts simultaneously. Loses the cockpit "everything at a glance" feel.
C
Carousel + Thumbnails
Row of mini sparkline thumbnails at top for at-a-glance status. Click any to expand as the main chart. One large chart visible at a time with full detail.
