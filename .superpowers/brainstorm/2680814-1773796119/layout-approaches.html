A. Insight Cards Grid

Replace the 3 stats cards with a 2x3 grid of insight cards. Each card answers one question with a headline metric + mini sparkline. Existing chart and breakdown stay below.

Pros

  • All 6 insights visible at once
  • Scannable — glance and know status
  • Minimal disruption to existing layout

Cons

  • Cards compete for space vertically
  • Mini visualizations may feel cramped

B. Tabbed Sections

Three tabs: Productivity (efficiency, velocity, sessions), Workflow (projects, activity, learning), Usage (current rate limits, tokens, breakdown). Each tab has its own stats + contextual chart.

Pros

  • More space per section — bigger charts
  • Charts change per tab — always relevant
  • Clean separation of concerns

Cons

  • Can't see all insights at once
  • Adds click overhead to switch views

C. Compact Hybrid

Top row = 3 headline metrics with status dots. Middle = unified chart with toggleable overlays (extends existing pattern). Bottom row = 3 compact insight widgets (activity heatmap, project bar, learning dots). Everything visible at once.

Pros

  • All insights on one screen, no tabs
  • Extends existing toggle-pills pattern
  • Information-dense but organized by row

Cons

  • Compact visualizations need careful design
  • May feel busy if not well-spaced
